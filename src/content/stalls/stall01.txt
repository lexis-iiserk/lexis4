Welcome to the Book Exchange stall at Lexis 4.0! Have old books gathering dust on your shelf? Bring them here and trade them for someone else’s treasured reads. It’s the perfect way to refresh your collection while sharing stories with fellow literature lovers. Whether you’re looking for classics, contemporary novels, or something entirely unexpected, the Book Exchange is your chance to discover new favorites while giving your own books a new home.
