Step into the Joystick Junction, where nostalgia meets excitement! Relive the golden era of arcade gaming with a handpicked selection of classic retro games that defined a generation. Whether you're flying solo or challenging your friends in intense multiplayer battles, we've got something for everyone! From pixel-perfect platformers to old-school beat 'em ups, our stall will transport you back in time to the days of 8-bit heroes and joystick triumphs. Ready to take on legendary levels or claim the high score? Power up and let the games begin!
